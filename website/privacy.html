TermScope Privacy Policy

Last updated July 15, 2026

TermScope analyzes Terms of Use and Privacy Policy documents only after a user clicks the TermScope shield or extension icon.

Information processed

TermScope sends the selected policy text, policy URL, policy type, and the user’s selected analysis preferences to the TermScope API. It does not intentionally collect passwords, form entries, payment information, private messages, or unrelated webpage content.

AI processing

The TermScope API uses Cloudflare Workers AI to identify concerning clauses and return a plain language explanation. Automated summaries may be inaccurate and are not legal advice.

Storage

This initial version does not create user accounts and does not intentionally store policy text or analysis results on the server. Cloudflare may process technical request logs according to its own service policies.

Contact

Questions can be submitted through the project’s GitHub repository.
